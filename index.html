Want to Play with Us?

When we play:

Open play is 7:30-9:30 p.m. Tuesdays and Fridays.
+ League Play. Check out our rules.

Where we play:

Bancroft Middle School & Contreras High School
+ Travel Leagues.

How to join:

Come to CHS on Tuesday or BMS on Friday. Bring your shoes and your game.
Don't be shy. New players join weekly, and we accomodate all skill levels from beginners to former college athletes.
Anyone is welcome. Anyone can be challenged.

About Lambda

Mission Statement

Lambda Basketball is an integrated and all-inclusive organization dedicated to providing the Los Angeles area LGBT community and its friends with a well-organized and friendly sporting environment. We are committed to uniting athletes, communities and cultures on and off the court while promoting health education and community service.
