Want to play with us?

We welcome new players at open gym and our seasonal leagues. Check out the information below or Contact Us if you have questions.

Open Gym

Open Gym is our version of pick-up/scrimmage night where we run 5 on 5, eight minute games. These nights are open to players of all skill levels, unless otherwise noted on the calendar.

Come in with a reversible jersey (or dark and light shirts), sign a waiver, pay $5 and you're good to go! One of our Lambda Basketball Board of Directors will be at the score table to help you get started.

League

Lambda Basketball operates 2 league seasons per year in the Spring and Fall. The season is generally 10-13 games and costs $100 which covers costs for the gym, referees, a reversible jersey, draft party, and a closing party. Teams of 8 are Captained by volunteer players in the league and consist of all skill levels.

Travel Teams

Lambda Basketball is proud to have many teams from the Los Angeles area represent us at various tournaments around the country and internationally. If you are interested in playing on a travel team, please email us and we'll help you get in contact with a team that is looking to add players.

About Lambda

Mission Statement

Lambda Basketball is an integrated and all-inclusive organization dedicated to providing the Los Angeles area LGBT community and its friends with a well-organized and friendly sporting environment. We are committed to uniting athletes, communities and cultures on and off the court while promoting health education and community service. Lambda is a subsidiary of Community OUTreach Alliance (COA).
